amrSolve

amrSolve is designed to be a small, robust, accurate, scalable, and efficient parallel elliptic linear solver for use in structured AMR codes in general, and Enzo in particular. It should be especially suitable for computing gravitational potential fields, and as a linear solver for implicit radiation transport methods.
